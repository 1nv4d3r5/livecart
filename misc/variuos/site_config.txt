Konfiguracinis failiukas yra
"application/configuration/config_values.config.xml"

<Config>
	<Key>productImagesDir</Key>			
	<Value>upload/images/products/</Value>
	<Category>default</Category>
	<Type></Type>			
</Config>

Kiekviena konfiguracinis elementas turi "key", "value", "type", "category", "description".
"Type" apibûdina konfiguracinio elemento tipà. Gali bûti: "string", "numeric", "integer", "bool" ir "list". Priklausomai nuo "type" Configuration kontroleryje parenkamas formos elementas, tai pat atintinkami validatoriai ir filtrai. Jei tipas list, tai parenkama ið selecto. Jo reiksmes aprasomos taip:
<Items>
	<Item>Vienas</Item>
	<Item>Du</Item>
	<Item>Trys</Item>
	<Item>Keturi</Item>			
</Items>

"category" reikalinga configuration kontroleryje formos kurimui, kad elementai bûtø sugrupuoti. 

Config gavimas programiskai:
<?php

	Configuration::getInstance->getValue("raktas");

?>

Jei kaþkas neaiðku þiûrëti ConfigurationController.

Config.xml pavizdys:

<ConfigFile>
	<Config>
		<Key>productImagesDir</Key>			
		<Value>upload/images/products/</Value>
		<Category>default</Category>	
		<Type>string</Type>	
		<Description></Description>	
	</Config>
	<Config>
		<Key>listTest</Key>			
		<Value>Du</Value>
		<Items>
			<Item>Vienas</Item>
			<Item>Du</Item>
			<Item>Trys</Item>
			<Item>Keturi</Item>			
		</Items>
		<Category>product</Category>	
		<Type>list</Type>
		<Description></Description>		
	</Config>
	<Config>
		<Key>maxProductImagesCount</Key>			
		<Value>5</Value>
		<Category>product</Category>	
		<Type>integer</Type>
		<Description>Maximal count of images of product</Description>		
	</Config>
	<Config>
		<Key>boolTest</Key>			
		<Value>1</Value>
		<Category>product</Category>	
		<Type>bool</Type>
		<Description>Test</Description>
	</Config>
</ConfigFile>
