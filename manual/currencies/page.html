Add Currency

To add a new currency simply click Add currency and choose a currency from the list followed by ADD button.

Set the Default Currency

The default currency is used as a main currency of your system, that is... For instance, all the other currency rates are calculated in respect with the default currency.

Adjust Rates

To adjust rates of you (active) currencies click on the Adjust Rates tab to proceed to manual rates setting. To set rates simply enter currency's value in the field provided. (save). See Automatic Adjust for keeping your rates up-to-date.

Automaticaly Adjust Rates

To configure automatic currency updater go to Options section. In the Options section you can choose an update frequency as well as update engine to use...

Enable or Disable Currencies

All the currencies are disabled by default, to enable a currency click it's checkbox by the currency name.

Sort Currencies

Sorting currencies determines the way all your system's active currencies will be displayed in the users currency switching menu. To set an appropriate currency order, click on the currency and drag it up or down.

Delete

To delete a currency, move your mouse over the language and click "delete" icon on the left.
