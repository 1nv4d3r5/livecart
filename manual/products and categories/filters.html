How do Filters work?

Each category has a set of filters assigned to it, for example, Monitors may have the following filter: screen size, type (LCD/CRT), response time, etc. Each of there filters may be selected by the cutomer in his/her search and combined together. Our solution doesn’t force the customer to select the product in pre-set order, instead – the customer may filter the products by any attribute he likes at any point of the process.
