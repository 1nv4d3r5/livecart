Products and categories

Manage Products and categories

As in most e-commerce stores, with LC you can manage your products by categories. Categories can also contain subcategories which, in turn, may have their own subcategories. The number of subcategories is unlimited.

Category Tree

Category tree represents how all of the categories are arranged. At the top of the tree is the Root category which is a parent category for all the descending categories. In the category tree you can easily manage your categories by selecting one of the options:

  • Create a new sub-category
  • Remove category
  • Move Category UP/DOWN
  • Viewing content?
